## What is Levvo?

Levvo is a shared WhatsApp inbox for sales teams.

A single place where your team handles customers, every conversation has an owner, and the owner sees exactly what’s happening in real-time — without having to ask.

## Who is it for?

For business owners with 2 to 10 people responding to sales via WhatsApp who need to know what’s going on without depending on status updates.

- Real estate agencies and property managers

## Two modes of use

**One number for the whole team**
The team responds from a single centralized number. Every conversation is assigned to a representative. The owner sees everything from the dashboard.

**Each representative with their own number**
Every salesperson connects their own WhatsApp. They can keep using it normally. Conversations are centralized in the owner's dashboard.

## FAQ

**Does the team have to change how they use WhatsApp?**
Not necessarily. There are two modes — with a centralized number or connecting each representative's WhatsApp. The business decides.

**Can the owner write in the conversations?**
Yes. Without the conversation being assigned to them. A record remains that they wrote it.

**What happens if two representatives respond to the same conversation?**
The assignment goes to the first one who replied. The second one is logged. Nothing is lost.

**My salesperson left and took the contacts. Is there a solution?**
Yes. With Levvo, conversations stay with the business number, not on the salesperson's phone. When someone leaves, the history stays.

**How much does it cost?**
$12 USD per user per month.
