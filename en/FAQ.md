## General Questions

**What is Levvo?**
Levvo is a shared WhatsApp inbox for sales teams. Multiple representatives respond to the same number from one place, and the business owner sees in real-time who handled each conversation.

**What type of business is Levvo for?**
For business owners with several people responding to WhatsApp: Real estate agencies, dealerships, distributors, shops, and service companies in LATAM.

**How much does it cost?**
$12 USD per user per month (starting with 3 users).

**Is there a trial period?**
Yes. You can start at [levvoapp.com](https://levvoapp.com) without a credit card.

## About Configuration

**How is WhatsApp connected to Levvo?**
You create your account, scan a QR code with your WhatsApp number, and that's it. The same process as WhatsApp Web.

**How long does it take to set up?**
The team can be operating on the same day. Creating an account, scanning the QR, and inviting representatives takes minutes.

## Daily Use

**What happens if I already use a CRM?**
Levvo is designed to be the layer that manages WhatsApp before it even goes to a CRM, or to replace a CRM if you only need it to organize sales conversations.

**Is it safe?**
Yes. We use the official WhatsApp connection through the same technology as WhatsApp Web. Your data and conversations are protected.
