## In 4 steps

**1. Create your account**
Visit levvoapp.com and register your business. No credit card required.

**2. Connect your WhatsApp**
Scan a QR code with the number you already use. Same process as WhatsApp Web. Your number is connected in less than 5 minutes.

**3. Invite your team**
Send the invitation link to your team members. Each one signs in and starts seeing conversations. Any member can invite others — not just the owner.

**4. Start seeing who responds to what**
In every conversation, the name of the representative who replied appears. The owner has complete visibility of the team from the dashboard — without asking any questions.

---

## What the representative sees

The chat module looks like WhatsApp Web. Conversations arrive there. Each representative sees the ones assigned to them and can respond with everything they already know — files, voice notes, emojis, quoting messages.

When a message arrives unassigned, the first representative to respond takes it. It is automatically registered in their name.

Tools available for the representative:
- **Internal notes** — messages visible only to the team, not to the client.
- **AI Copilot** — summarizes the entire conversation and suggests what to respond before typing.
- **Tags** — to organize conversations by type, status, or priority.
- **Export conversation** — in Markdown format when needed.

---

## What the owner sees

The owner has access to everything without conversations being assigned to them. They can write in any chat — it simply remains registered that they intervened.

From the dashboard, the owner sees:
- Active conversations per representative.
- Conversations without a responsible party.
- Volume and response times per representative.
- Analysis of how the team responded — without manually asking for reports.

---

## How automatic assignment works

When a new message arrives, the AI classifier enters first.

1. Greets the customer.
2. Detects the topic — sales, support, price inquiry, etc.
3. Assigns the conversation to the representative assigned to that topic.
4. If no representative is available for that topic, it remains unassigned for the team to pick up.

The owner never receives automatic assignments.

---

## Two modes of operation

**Mode A — One number for the whole team**
A single WhatsApp number. The whole team responds from Levvo. Each conversation has a responsible party. The owner sees everything centralized.

**Mode B — Each representative with their own number**
Each salesperson connects their personal WhatsApp to Levvo. They can keep using it normally from their phone. Conversations still arrive at the owner's dashboard.

Key difference in Mode B: the representative responding from their personal WhatsApp does not have access to the copilot. The one responding from Levvo does.

---

## What happens when a representative leaves

Conversations stay with the business number, not on the representative's phone. A new team member enters, sees the complete history, and continues where it left off — without losing context or contacts.
