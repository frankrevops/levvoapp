## General & Business

**What is Levvo exactly?**
Levvo is an "Operational Coordination Layer" for WhatsApp. It allows multiple sales reps to use the same WhatsApp number (or separate lines) with centralized management, AI assistance, and total owner visibility.

**Is this for any business?**
It is optimized for sales-driven teams: Real Estate, Dealerships, Professional Services, and B2B SaaS teams who rely on WhatsApp to close deals.

**How much does it cost?**
Our base plan starts at $12 USD per user/month. We believe in transparent, flat pricing without "per-conversation" or "per-message" hidden fees.

---

## Security & Reliability

**Is Levvo safe?**
Yes. We use the same connection protocol as WhatsApp Web. We do not store your contacts' personal phone data on our servers beyond what's necessary for the coordination layer. Your data is encrypted and secure.

**Will my number get blocked?**
Levvo is designed for Inbound Sales teams responding to customer demand. As long as you follow WhatsApp's anti-spam guidelines (not sending thousands of messages to strangers), the direct connection model is highly stable and secure.

**What happens if I want to stop using Levvo?**
You simply disconnect the direct phone link. Since your number was never migrated to complex systems, you can go back to using the standard WhatsApp app in seconds without losing anything.

## Technical & Features

**Does Levvo replace my CRM?**
Levvo can replace a CRM for teams that close everything through chat. For larger companies, Levvo acts as the "Chat Ops" layer that handles the fast-paced conversation before the data is recorded in a long-term CRM like HubSpot or Salesforce.

**How does the AI Copilot work?**
The AI analyzes the context of the current conversation and your historical interactions. It then provides suggestions for the "best next reply" to help your reps close faster. It never sends a message without human approval.

**Can I use multiple WhatsApp numbers in one workspace?**
Yes. Levvo supports multiple numbers (Multi-Line mode) so you can have a "Sales" number and a "Support" number, or simply see all your individual reps' lines in one place.

---

## Privacy & Data Storage

**Does Levvo store my conversations permanently?**
No. Levvo is designed with "Privacy by Design" principles. We only maintain a **60-day operational cache** for active coordination. After 60 days, data is automatically purged from our systems. Your history always remains on your original physical phone.

**Is Levvo GDPR compliant?**
Yes. By avoiding the creation of permanent historical databases and long-term user profiling, we ensure compliance and minimize data risk. The control of personal data remains with the business on their own terminal.

**What if I need to keep logs for longer?**
You can export any conversation to **Markdown** format at any time for your records. For multimedia (photos, videos), Levvo allows you to connect your own external storage (like Amazon S3 or Cloudflare R2), so you maintain total ownership of your files.
