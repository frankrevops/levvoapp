This is the nightmare scenario for many service businesses in the UK. A salesperson builds a great book of business on their personal WhatsApp, then resigns and starts messaging those same clients for their new employer. Without a central system, you have no way to stop it.

Levvo acts as a **safety net for your client relationships**. By ensuring that team conversations happen through a managed platform, the "relationship" is with the brand, not just the individual. ## Recovery and prevention questions:

**Is it too late if my rep has already left?**
If you weren't using a managed system like Levvo, it’s hard to recover those specific chats. This is why thousands of businesses are switching *before* they lose another team member. Levvo is your "Insurance Policy" for your sales pipeline.

**Does a shared inbox make the service feel less personal?**
Not at all. In fact, it makes it *more* professional. When a client messages, they get a reply from "The Company," but with the personality of the agent currently assigned. If that agent changes, the context travels with the client, ensuring they never have to repeat themselves.

**How do I enforce the use of a company-managed WhatsApp?**
By making it easier for the rep. Levvo’s desktop interface is so much better for typing and managing 20+ leads that reps *prefer* using it over their phones. They get to be more productive, and you get peace of mind.
