## Why successful sales teams move to Levvo

If your team uses one WhatsApp number with multiple reps, or if each rep uses their own line, you've likely faced **"The WhatsApp Chaos"**:

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**Does my team need to change how they work?**
No. Levvo is designed to be adopted in minutes. If your team can use WhatsApp Web, they can use Levvo. No complex onboarding or training required.

**How is it different from other complex systems?**
Levvo works by connecting your phone directly (like you do with your computer). This means you keep your physical phone, you don't pay extra per-conversation fees, and you get up and running in 5 minutes instead of weeks.

**What happens if a rep leaves?**
Your business keeps the data. The conversation history and contacts are registered in your workspace. You simply assign the chats to a new rep, and they continue the sale with the full context.

**Can managers intervene in chats?**
Yes. Managers can view any conversation and intervene if necessary. All interventions are logged, ensuring high-quality standards across the board.
