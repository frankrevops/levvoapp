# Shared WhatsApp Inbox for Sales Teams

Multiple salespeople, one single number, and no one knows who responded to what.
Customers wait. Leads get cold. The owner sees nothing.

**Why it happens:** WhatsApp Business was not designed for team coordination. It doesn't assign conversations, doesn't record who replied, and doesn't give visibility to the owner. When more than one person is answering the same number, everything depends on someone remembering to notify the others.

**What changes with Levvo:**
- Every conversation has a responsible person visible to the whole team.
- The owner sees in real-time who handled each customer — without asking.
- No one replies twice to the same customer or leaves a chat unattended.
