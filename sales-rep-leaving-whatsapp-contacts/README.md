# What happens to your clients if your top sales rep leaves tomorrow?

Most UK businesses realise too late that their most valuable asset—their customer database—is sitting in a sales rep's personal phone. If that rep leaves, your contacts, your negotiation history, and your future revenue walk out the door with them.

### How Levvo solves this:
Levvo ensures that **your business owns the customer relationship**, not the individual agent. By centralising communication into a managed workspace, every conversation is recorded on your company's "ground truth." If a rep leaves, you simply reassign their chats to someone else, who can see the full history and continue the sale without missing a beat.

---

## Security questions for business owners:

**How do I stop my data from leaving on a personal device?**
When your team uses Levvo, conversations happen within your business workspace. You maintain a searchable operational history of every interaction, protecting your brand's knowledge and your pipeline even if a rep's physical phone isn't present.

**Is it awkward to move reps from personal to company lines?**
It doesn't have to be. With Levvo's "Multi-Line" mode, reps can keep using their existing numbers while you get centralised visibility. It’s the perfect balance of agent freedom and company security.

**Can I export my chat history if I need to?**
Yes. Levvo allows you to export conversations in Markdown or other formats for your CRM or legal records. You are never "locked in" to a device; you are always in control of your data.
