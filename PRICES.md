## Pricing FAQ

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. You can start using Levvo for free without a credit card to see if it fits your team's workflow.

**What happens if I need more than 20 users?**
Levvo scales with you. The cost remains linear per additional user, keeping your budget predictable.

**Is it a monthly or annual contract?**
We offer flexible monthly billing with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time through our settings, according to our terms of service (payments for the current period are non-refundable).
