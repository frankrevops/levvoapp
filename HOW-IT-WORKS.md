# How Levvo Works: Speed, Coordination and AI

[🇪🇸 Versión en Español](../COMO-FUNCIONA.md)

Levvo is built for speed. We believe that a sales team should be able to organize their WhatsApp in minutes, not months. Here is how we turn your chat volume into a structured sales machine.

---

## 4 Steps to Total Coordination

**1. Instant Account Creation**
Sign up at levvoapp.com/en. No credit card required. You get immediate access to your workspace.

**2. Link your phone directly**
Connect your phone in seconds using the same method you use for your computer. Your connection is stable, encrypted, and keeps your number on your physical device.

**3. Invite & Assign**
Send a Magic Link to your team. As they join, you can assign them to specific roles or topics. In minutes, everyone is seeing the conversations they need to see.

**4. Real-Time Supervision**
As messages arrive, you’ll see them being picked up and answered in real-time. The dashboard populates with metrics automatically. No more manual data entry.

## The Representative Experience (AI-Powered)

The Levvo inbox is designed for productivity. It feels familiar but adds "superpowers" that the standard app lacks:

*   **Internal Notes:** Chat with your team "behind the scenes" without the customer seeing.
*   **AI Copilot:** Summarizes the entire history of a lead and suggests the next reply based on previous successful closes.
*   **Smart Labels:** Filter by "Lead Hot", "Follow-up Required", or "Closed" to focus your energy where the money is.
*   **Markdown Export:** Export any conversation record for legal compliance or CRM documentation.

---

## The Manager Experience (Data-Driven)

Managers no longer have to guess. Our dashboard provides a 360-degree view of the sales floor:

*   **Load Balancing:** See who is overloaded with chats and reassign them with one click.
*   **Health Indicator:** Real-time alerts when a lead waits too long for a reply.
*   **Historical Analysis:** Export team performance reports in CSV/Markdown to find your top-performing scripts.
*   **Live Audit:** Jump into any chat to coach a rep or close a difficult deal yourself.

---

## Intelligent Routing (AI Assistant)

Levvo includes an optional AI Assistant that acts as your "front desk":

1.  **Friendly Greeting:** Greets the customer instantly.
2.  **Intent Detection:** Identifies if the user wants "Pricing", "Support", or "Sales".
3.  **Automatic Assignment:** Routes the chat to the correct specialist immediately.
4.  **Handoff:** The rep enters the chat with the "Intent" already visible, saving 2-3 minutes of discovery questions.

---

## Built for Scalability

Whether you have 3 reps or 30, Levvo scales without adding friction. If a rep leaves the company, the contacts and the history stay in the business line. Your brand's relationship with the customer remains unbroken.
